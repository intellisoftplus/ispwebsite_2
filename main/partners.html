Partners

Google Partnership

Google For Work

We have partnered up with Google to provide world class services to businesses. With a guarantee of 99% uptime and a suite of Google products ranging from Gmail, Drive and Calendar we offer to enterprises the ability to share, collaborate and communicate within the organisation and outside the organisation in a secure, fast and simple manner

Google For Education

Intellisoft has partnered with Google under the Education Go Digital Programme, an initiative aimed at uplifting Education in Africa through the use of technology tools. we have overseen the implementation of Google Apps for over 50,000 higher learning institutions, staff and students in Kenya alone. Through this initiative, students and teachers have access to a platform that makes it easier for them to communicate and collaborate promoting ICT skills development and technology flag bearers in the next generation of the kenyan workforce

Microsoft Partnership

Microsoft Office

Microsoft is Globally recognised brand with a wide reach here in Kenya and we have partnered up to provide world class services tailor made for small and medium enterprises. We use our expertise to develop solutions within the Microsoft environment to solve business problems that includes but not limited to custom applications that integrate with the office suite and in house applications
